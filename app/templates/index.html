Real-Time Face Recognition

Welcome! This web application captures frames from your webcam, sends them to the server for face detection and recognition, and displays the annotated results on top of your live video feed.

To add (or "tag") yourself or someone else, position a face clearly in front of the camera, then click the "Tag This Face" button and type a name. The system will store this embedding and label. Next time this person appears, their name should show up (provided recognition is confident enough).

Please ensure you grant camera access in your browser if prompted. For best results, use a well-lit environment and face the camera directly.
